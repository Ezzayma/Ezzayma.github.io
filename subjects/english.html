The subject of "English" primarily involves the study of the English language, its grammar, and its usage, as well as the exploration of English literature and writing. It focuses on developing key skills in reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension, enabling students to communicate effectively and critically analyze texts. English literature includes the study of classic and modern works from authors like Shakespeare, Jane Austen, and contemporary writers. Through the study of English, students engage with diverse genres, historical contexts, and cultural perspectives, fostering both linguistic proficiency and a deeper appreciation of literary expression.
