The subject of "Systems and Networks" focuses on the design, implementation, and management of computer systems and communication networks. It covers topics such as computer architecture, operating systems, network protocols, data transmission, and network security. Students learn how systems operate, how devices connect and communicate within networks, and how to maintain the performance, reliability, and security of these infrastructures. This subject is essential for understanding how modern technologies work together to support everything from personal devices to large-scale internet operations, and it prepares students for roles in IT, cybersecurity, and systems engineering.
