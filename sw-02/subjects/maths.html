The subject of "Maths" (Mathematics) involves the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, patterns, and their relationships. It encompasses a wide range of topics such as arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics. In mathematics, students learn to solve problems, analyze data, and develop logical reasoning and critical thinking skills. The subject forms the foundation for many fields including science, engineering, economics, and technology. By studying maths, students gain tools to understand both abstract concepts and real-world applications, making it essential for everyday life and advanced academic pursuits.
