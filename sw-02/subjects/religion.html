La Rocca Maria

The subject of "Religion" explores the beliefs, practices, history, and cultural impact of various religious traditions around the world. It involves the study of major world religions such as Christianity, Islam, Hinduism, Buddhism, Judaism, and others, examining their core teachings, rituals, texts, and philosophies. Additionally, the subject may address ethical and moral questions, the role of religion in society, and interfaith dialogue. Through this study, students gain a deeper understanding of spiritual beliefs, cultural diversity, and the ways religion shapes human experiences and worldviews across different societies.
